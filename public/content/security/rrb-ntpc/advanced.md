# RRB NTPC Advanced Preparation

## Exam Pattern — Tier 1 and Tier 2

### Tier 1 (CBT 1): 100 Questions, 90 Minutes
- Mathematics: 30 questions
- General Intelligence and Reasoning: 30 questions
- General Awareness: 40 questions
- Negative marking: -1/3 per wrong answer

### Tier 2 (CBT 2): 120 Questions, 90 Minutes
- Mathematics: 35 questions
- General Intelligence and Reasoning: 35 questions
- General Awareness: 50 questions
- Higher standard questions; more current affairs

## Railway-Specific General Knowledge

### Indian Railways Facts
- Largest employer in India (13+ lakh employees)
- Zones: 18 railway zones
- Rajdhani, Shatabdi, Duronto, Vande Bharat Express trains
- Konkan Railway: highest rail bridge
- Pamban Bridge: sea bridge connecting Rameswaram

### Key Railway Terms for GK
- DFC (Dedicated Freight Corridor): Eastern and Western DFC
- RVNL: Rail Vikas Nigam Limited
- IRCTC: booking and tourism
- RDSO: Research Designs & Standards Organisation (Lucknow)
- NMRC, DMRC, MMRC: metro rail corporations

## Advanced Mathematics for NTPC

### Algebra Shortcuts
```
If a + b + c = 0, then a³+b³+c³ = 3abc
If a + b = s, ab = p: a²+b² = s²-2p; a³+b³ = s³-3sp
Age problems: form equations; check options backwards
```

### Geometry (High Yield in NTPC)
- Pythagoras triplets: 3-4-5, 5-12-13, 8-15-17, 7-24-25
- Angle in semicircle = 90°
- Tangent-radius perpendicular
- Exterior angle = sum of two non-adjacent interior angles
- For triangle: incircle radius r = Area/Semi-perimeter

### Profit and Loss Advanced
```
Dishonest trader who sells at CP but uses false weight:
Gain% = (True weight - False weight)/False weight × 100

Selling at x% above CP to get y% gain after z% discount:
x = (100+y)/(100-z) × 100 - 100
```
