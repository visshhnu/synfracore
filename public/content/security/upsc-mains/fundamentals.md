# UPSC Mains Fundamentals

## Overview
UPSC Civil Services Main Examination - written stage for IAS/IPS/IFS selection.

## Exam Pattern
- Multiple choice questions with negative marking
- Tests General Intelligence, General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude, English Language
- Conducted by central recruitment boards

## General Intelligence and Reasoning
- **Analogies**: Word analogies (Dog:Kennel :: Bird:?)
- **Series**: Number series, letter series, alpha-numeric
- **Classification**: Odd one out
- **Coding-Decoding**: Letter/number substitutions
- **Blood Relations**: Family tree problems
- **Directions**: N/S/E/W navigation problems
- **Syllogism**: Logical deduction from premises
- **Matrix**: 3x3 grid pattern completion

## Quantitative Aptitude

### Number System
- Types of numbers: natural, whole, integers, rational, irrational, real
- Divisibility rules: 2 (last digit even), 3 (digit sum ÷3), 4 (last 2 digits ÷4), 9 (digit sum ÷9)
- LCM and HCF methods: prime factorization, division method
- Fractions, decimals, percentage conversions

### Arithmetic
- **Percentage**: change = (diff/original) × 100
- **Profit and Loss**: Profit% = (Profit/CP) × 100; SP = CP(1+P/100)
- **Simple Interest**: SI = PRT/100; CI = P(1+R/100)^T - P
- **Ratio and Proportion**: a:b = c:d → ad = bc
- **Time, Speed, Distance**: D = S×T; Average speed for equal distances = 2S₁S₂/(S₁+S₂)
- **Work and Time**: If A does in 'a' days, rate = 1/a; combined rate = sum of individual rates

### Algebra and Geometry
- Linear and quadratic equations
- Mensuration: areas and volumes of standard shapes
- Trigonometry ratios and identities

## General Awareness

### History
- Ancient India: Indus Valley, Vedic Age, Maurya, Gupta empires
- Medieval India: Delhi Sultanate, Mughal Empire, Bhakti/Sufi movements
- Modern India: British rule, freedom struggle, independence

### Geography
- Physical features: Himalayas, peninsular plateau, rivers, coastline
- Climate: monsoon, seasons, rainfall patterns
- Economic geography: agriculture, industries, minerals

### Polity
- Constitution: Fundamental Rights, Directive Principles, Fundamental Duties
- Parliament, President, Supreme Court
- State governments, Panchayati Raj

### Economy
- Budget, GDP, inflation, unemployment
- RBI and monetary policy
- Five-year plans and NITI Aayog

## English Language
- Grammar: tenses, prepositions, articles, subject-verb agreement
- Vocabulary: synonyms, antonyms, idioms, phrases
- Comprehension passages
- Sentence correction and rearrangement
