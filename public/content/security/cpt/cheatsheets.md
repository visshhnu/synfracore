## MDM Quick Score Guide
| MDM Level | Problems | Data | Risk |
|----------|---------|------|------|
| Straightforward | 1 self-limited | None/minimal | OTC meds |
| Low | 2 self-limited OR 1 stable chronic | Lab/imaging ordered | Rx drugs |
| Moderate | 1 uncontrolled OR new problem | Independent interpretation | Rx drug mgmt |
| High | 1+ severe threat to life/limb | Extensive | Drug threatening life / hospitalization |
