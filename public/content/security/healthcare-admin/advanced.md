# Healthcare Administration Advanced Topics

## Value-Based Care Models

### ACO (Accountable Care Organization)
Groups of providers who share responsibility for quality and cost of care for a defined population.
- MSSP (Medicare Shared Savings Program): ACOs can share in savings vs benchmark
- Enhanced Track: Two-sided risk (upside + downside)
- REACH Model: Replaces GPDC model; higher risk/reward

### PCMH (Patient-Centered Medical Home)
Primary care model focusing on coordinated, comprehensive care.
NCQA PCMH recognition standards: team-based care, care management, quality improvement.
Typically receives per-member per-month (PMPM) care management payment.

### Bundled Payments (BPCI)
Single payment for episode of care (e.g., hip replacement — hospital + rehab + follow-up).
CMS BPCI Advanced: voluntary participation; risk sharing.
Incentivizes coordinated, efficient care across settings.

## Advanced Revenue Cycle

### Denial Management Metrics
- First-pass resolution rate: claims paid on first submission (target >90%)
- Denial rate: % of claims denied (target <5%)
- Denial overturn rate: % of appealed denials that are won
- Days in AR: average collection time (target <50 days)
- Net collection rate: collections vs expected net revenue (target >95%)

### Root Cause Analysis for Denials
| Denial Category | Root Cause | Prevention |
|----------------|-----------|-----------|
| Eligibility | Coverage verification not done | Real-time eligibility check |
| Authorization | Prior auth not obtained | Auth tracking system |
| Coding | Wrong codes or sequencing | CDI, coder education |
| Timely filing | Claim submitted late | Claims tracking, EDI |
| Medical necessity | Insufficient documentation | CDI program |

## Population Health Management

### Disease Management Programs
- Chronic condition registries: identify all patients with diabetes, HTN, COPD
- Care gap closure: patients overdue for screenings or follow-ups
- High-risk patient identification: frequent ED visits, multiple admissions
- Care management interventions: care coordinators, telephonic outreach

### SDOH (Social Determinants of Health)
- Housing, food security, transportation, education, income
- Z codes document SDOH in ICD-10-CM
- Screening tools: PRAPARE, AHC HRSN screening tool
- Community health workers address SDOH barriers

## Healthcare Mergers and Consolidation

### Trends
- Health system consolidation: vertical (hospital + physician group) and horizontal (hospital + hospital)
- Payer consolidation: fewer larger insurance companies
- Impact: potential efficiency gains but also reduced competition

### Regulatory Oversight
- FTC (Federal Trade Commission): antitrust review of mergers
- CMS approval for certain ownership changes
- State attorneys general review healthcare mergers

## Emergency Preparedness

### CMS Emergency Preparedness Rule
- All CMS-certified providers must have emergency preparedness plans
- Four core elements: risk assessment/planning, policies and procedures, communication plan, training and testing
- Annual review required; exercises (drills) required

## Healthcare IT Governance

### HITRUST Framework
- Information security framework for healthcare
- Incorporates HIPAA, NIST, ISO standards
- Used by payers and large health systems for vendor management

### Interoperability Rules (21st Century Cures Act)
- Information blocking prohibited
- FHIR-based patient access APIs required
- Patients can access their own data via apps
