## Important Equations
```
PV = nRT   (ideal gas)
ΔG = ΔH - TΔS   (Gibbs)
ΔG° = -RT ln K
E = E° - (0.0592/n) log Q   (Nernst at 25°C)
rate = k[A]^m[B]^n
ln(k₂/k₁) = (Ea/R)(1/T₁ - 1/T₂)   (Arrhenius)
pH = pKa + log([A⁻]/[HA])   (Henderson-Hasselbalch)
```

## Periodic Trends
- Atomic radius: ↑ down group, ↓ across period
- IE₁: ↓ down group, ↑ across period (exceptions: Group 2>3, Group 5>6)
- Electronegativity: F highest; ↑ across period, ↓ down group
- Electron affinity: generally ↑ across period, ↓ down group
