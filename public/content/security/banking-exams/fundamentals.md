# Banking Exams — Fundamentals

## Overview of Banking Exams in India

Banking sector exams are conducted by IBPS, SBI, and RBI to recruit officers, clerks, and specialists. These are highly competitive with lakhs of applicants.

## Major Banking Exams

| Exam | Conducting Body | Posts |
|------|----------------|-------|
| IBPS PO | IBPS | Probationary Officers |
| IBPS Clerk | IBPS | Clerical Cadre |
| IBPS SO | IBPS | Specialist Officers |
| SBI PO | SBI | SBI Probationary Officers |
| SBI Clerk | SBI | Junior Associates |
| RBI Grade B | RBI | Officers Grade B |
| NABARD Grade A | NABARD | Development Assistants |

## Subject-Wise Preparation

### 1. Quantitative Aptitude Topics
- Number System, Simplification, Approximation
- Percentage, Profit & Loss, SI/CI
- Ratio & Proportion, Averages, Mixtures
- Time & Work, Time Speed Distance
- Data Interpretation (Tables, Charts, Graphs)
- Quadratic Equations, Number Series

### 2. Reasoning Ability Topics
- Syllogisms, Coded Inequalities
- Seating Arrangement (Linear, Circular)
- Puzzles (Floor-based, Box-based)
- Blood Relations, Direction Sense
- Alphanumeric Series, Order & Ranking
- Input-Output, Coding-Decoding

### 3. English Language Topics
- Reading Comprehension
- Cloze Test, Fill in the Blanks
- Error Detection, Sentence Correction
- Para Jumbles, Sentence Completion
- Vocabulary (Synonyms, Antonyms)

### 4. General Awareness / Banking Awareness
- Banking History and Structure
- RBI, SEBI, IRDAI functions
- Government Schemes (PM Jan Dhan, PMMY)
- Current Affairs (last 6 months)
- Budget highlights, Economic indicators

## Study Plan — 3 Months
- Month 1: Basics of all subjects
- Month 2: Practice sectional mock tests
- Month 3: Full mock tests + current affairs revision

## Important Tips
- Attempt 3–5 full mock tests per week in final month
- Focus on accuracy over speed initially
- Current affairs: read newspapers daily, follow banking news
- Negative marking: -0.25 per wrong answer in most exams
- Sectional cutoffs exist — don't ignore any section
