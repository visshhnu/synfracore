# Personal Hygiene Cheatsheet

## Daily Hygiene Routine
```
MORNING:
☑ Brush teeth 2 min (fluoride toothpaste, soft bristles)
☑ Tongue cleaning (reduces bad breath)
☑ Shower (focus: armpits, groin, feet, face)
☑ Clean underwear and socks
☑ Deodorant/antiperspirant

THROUGHOUT THE DAY:
☑ Wash hands: before/after eating, after toilet, after coughing/blowing nose
☑ Avoid touching face unnecessarily
☑ Cough/sneeze into bent elbow (not hands)

EVENING:
☑ Brush teeth + floss (most critical session of day)
☑ Remove makeup completely
☑ Skincare routine
☑ Clean pyjamas (don't wear daytime clothes to bed)
```

## WHO 6-Step Handwashing Technique
```
Step 1: Palm to palm — rub
Step 2: Right over left interlaced — rub; repeat other side
Step 3: Palm to palm, fingers interlaced — rub
Step 4: Backs of fingers into opposing palms — rub
Step 5: Rotational rubbing of left thumb — repeat other side
Step 6: Rotational rubbing of fingertips into opposite palm — repeat
MINIMUM 20 SECONDS TOTAL
```

## Oral Hygiene Rules
- Brush: 2× daily, 2 minutes each session, 45° angle to gum line
- Floss: Once daily — more important than brushing for gum health
- Replace toothbrush: Every 3 months or after illness
- Dentist: Every 6 months for professional clean and check-up
