# HCPCS Level II Advanced Topics

## DME PDAC (Pricing, Data Analysis, and Coding)

### PDAC Verification
CMS PDAC contractor verifies that products meet HCPCS code definitions before being listed on coverage/reimbursement lists. Manufacturers submit products for PDAC verification.
Coders must use PDAC-verified codes when billing Medicare for DME.

## Wheelchair Coding Deep Dive

### Manual Wheelchairs
- K0001: Standard wheelchair
- K0002: Standard hemi-wheelchair
- K0003: Lightweight wheelchair
- K0004: High-strength lightweight
- K0005: Ultra-lightweight (<34 lbs)
- K0006-K0008: Heavy-duty/extra heavy-duty
- K0009: Other manual wheelchair base

### Power Wheelchairs
- K0800-K0899: Group 1-5 power wheelchairs
- Groups based on drive type, stability, performance
- Requires face-to-face evaluation by physician
- Prior authorization required for most power wheelchairs

### Scooters/Power Operated Vehicles (POV)
- K0010: Standard POV (3-4 wheel)
- K0011: Standard POV with captains chair
- K0014: Other POV
- Cannot ambulate to perform mobility-related activities of daily living

## Oxygen Equipment

### Stationary vs Portable
- E0424: Stationary concentrator
- E0431: Portable gaseous oxygen system
- E0433: Portable liquid oxygen system
- E0441-E0444: Oxygen contents (stationary/portable)

### Oxygen CMN Requirements
- Oxygen saturation documented (SpO2 ≤88% or PaO2 ≤55 mmHg at rest OR exercise-induced desaturation)
- Testing date and results on CMN
- Annual recertification required

## Ambulance Coding

### ALS vs BLS
- A0427: ALS Level 1 (non-emergency, advanced life support)
- A0428: BLS non-emergency
- A0429: BLS emergency
- A0430: Fixed wing air transport
- A0431: Rotary wing (helicopter)

### Origin-Destination Modifiers (Two-Letter Combination)
First letter = origin, Second letter = destination:
R=Residence, H=Hospital, N=Skilled Nursing Facility, G=Hospital-based
Example: -RH (residence to hospital), -NH (SNF to hospital)

## HCPCS Modifiers
- -GA: Waiver of liability statement on file
- -GX: Notice of liability issued voluntarily (non-covered service)
- -GY: Item is statutorily excluded (non-covered)
- -GZ: Expected to be denied as not reasonable/necessary
- -KX: Requirements specified in the LCD have been met
- -EY: No physician order on file for item

## Compliance in HCPCS Billing
- Billing for products not delivered or returned
- Billing expired CMNs without renewal
- Upcoding DME (billing higher-complexity item than supplied)
- Waiving copayments without documentation (inducement)
- All subject to Medicare fraud enforcement
