# ICD-10-CM Advanced Topics

## Mental Health Coding

### DSM-5 Aligned Codes
- Major depressive disorder, single episode: F32.0 (mild), F32.1 (moderate), F32.2 (severe without psychosis), F32.3 (severe with psychosis)
- Recurrent MDD: F33.0-F33.3 (parallel severity levels)
- GAD: F41.1 | Panic disorder: F41.0 | Social anxiety: F40.10
- PTSD: F43.10 (unspecified), F43.11 (acute <3 months), F43.12 (chronic ≥3 months)
- Bipolar I: F31.x (various episode types) | Bipolar II: F31.81
- Schizophrenia: F20.9 | Schizoaffective: F25.x

### Substance Use Disorders
- Mild: 4th character .1x; Moderate/Severe: .2x
- Alcohol: F10.1x (mild) or F10.2x (mod/severe)
- Opioid: F11.x | Cannabis: F12.x | Stimulant: F15.x
- "In sustained remission": code with appropriate character

### Insulin Use Coding
- Z79.4: Long-term (current) use of insulin
- Required with E11 (Type 2) codes when patient is on insulin
- NOT needed with E10 (Type 1) — insulin is assumed

## COVID-19 and Post-COVID Coding
- U07.1: COVID-19 confirmed (positive test or clinical confirmation)
- U09.9: Post-COVID condition, unspecified (residual effects after acute COVID resolves)
- Z20.822: Contact with or exposure to COVID-19 (no symptoms)
- Z86.16: Personal history of COVID-19
- M35.81: Multisystem inflammatory syndrome (MIS) associated with COVID-19

## Injury Coding — Complex Scenarios

### Multiple Injuries
- Code each injury separately
- Sequencing: most severe injury first, or injury requiring most resources
- Do NOT use T07 (unspecified multiple injuries) if individual injuries can be identified

### Fracture Coding Nuances
- Open vs closed: adds specificity (wound communicating with fracture)
- Displaced vs non-displaced: refers to bone fragment position
- 7th characters: A (initial), D (subsequent), G (delayed healing), K (nonunion), P (malunion), S (sequela)
- Pathological fractures (M84.5-): different coding than traumatic fractures

## Z Codes — Advanced Uses

### Mandatory Z Codes (Always Required)
- Z79.4: Insulin use with Type 2 DM
- Z79.01: Long-term anticoagulants
- Z68: BMI (must code E66 obesity first)
- Z87.891: Personal history of nicotine dependence (when no longer using)

### Social Determinants of Health (Z55-Z65)
- Z59.0: Homelessness | Z59.1: Inadequate housing
- Z59.4: Lack of adequate food | Z63.4: Disappearance of family member
- Z63.5: Disruption of family | Z56.0: Unemployment
- Required to report for value-based care and population health programs
