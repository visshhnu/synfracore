## What is Human Essentials?

Prevention is better than cure. Annual checkups, blood work, and screenings catch problems early. Learn to read your own lab reports. Know your baseline numbers: BP, blood glucose, cholesterol, BMI.

## Why Human Essentials?

Food is information for your body. Focus on whole foods, reduce ultra-processed foods. Hydration affects cognitive performance. Learn the difference between evidence-based nutrition and marketing.

---

## Learning Modules

### Module 01 — Health & Preventive Care
*Doctor guidance, preventive awareness*

Prevention is better than cure. Annual checkups, blood work, and screenings catch problems early. Learn to read your own lab reports. Know your baseline numbers: BP, blood glucose, cholesterol, BMI.

**Topics covered:**

- Annual health screenings — 🟢 Beginner
- Understanding lab reports — 🟡 Intermediate
- Medication awareness — 🟡 Intermediate

### Module 02 — Nutrition Fundamentals
*Evidence-based eating*

Food is information for your body. Focus on whole foods, reduce ultra-processed foods. Hydration affects cognitive performance. Learn the difference between evidence-based nutrition and marketing.

**Topics covered:**

- Macronutrients and micronutrients — 🟢 Beginner
- Hydration and electrolytes — 🟢 Beginner
- Anti-inflammatory foods — 🟡 Intermediate

### Module 03 — Mental Wellness
*Stress, sleep, mindfulness*

Mental wellness underpins physical health and professional performance. Sleep is non-negotiable — 7-9 hours affects cognitive function, immunity, and emotional regulation. Chronic stress damages health at the cellular level.

**Topics covered:**

- Sleep hygiene and sleep science — 🟢 Beginner
- Stress response and cortisol — 🟡 Intermediate
- Mindfulness practices — 🟢 Beginner

### Module 04 — Sustainability & Plastic-Free
*Conscious living*

Sustainable living is not about perfection — it is about conscious choices. Plastic-free starts with identifying single-use plastics in your routine. Each swap is progress. Environmental health connects to personal health.

**Topics covered:**

- Plastic-free living basics — 🟢 Beginner
- Sustainable consumption — 🟡 Intermediate
- Carbon footprint awareness — 🟡 Intermediate
