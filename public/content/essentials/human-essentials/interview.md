# Life Skills FAQ

**Q: What are the most important habits for long-term health?**
Sleep 7-9 hours consistently. Daily movement (150 min moderate exercise/week minimum). Real food diet (vegetables, protein, complex carbs — minimise ultra-processed). Stress management (meditation, social connection, time in nature). Hydration (2-3L water daily).

**Q: How to start building an emergency fund?**
Target 3-6 months of expenses. Start with ₹1,000 automatic transfer on salary day. Keep in high-interest savings (not current account). Do not invest emergency fund in markets — must be liquid.
