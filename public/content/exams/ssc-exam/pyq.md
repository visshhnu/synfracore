# SSC CGL/CHSL — Previous Year Pattern Questions

## Quantitative Aptitude

**Q1.** A shopkeeper marks his goods 25% above cost price and gives a discount of 10%. His profit%?
Solution: Let CP = 100. MP = 125. SP = 125 x 0.9 = 112.5. Profit = 12.5%
**Answer: 12.5%**

**Q2.** If 15 men can do a work in 12 days, how many men can do it in 9 days?
Solution: M1 x D1 = M2 x D2 → 15 x 12 = M2 x 9 → M2 = 20
**Answer: 20 men**

**Q3.** A train 200m long crosses a pole in 10 sec. Speed in km/h?
Speed = 200/10 = 20 m/s = 20 x 18/5 = **72 km/h**

**Q4.** The ratio of ages of A and B is 3:5. After 6 years ratio becomes 2:3. A's current age?
3x + 6)/(5x + 6) = 2/3 → 9x + 18 = 10x + 12 → x = 6. A = 3 x 6 = **18 years**

**Q5.** What is the compound interest on Rs 8000 at 10% p.a. for 2 years?
CI = 8000[(1.1)^2 - 1] = 8000[1.21 - 1] = 8000 x 0.21 = **Rs 1680**

## General Intelligence & Reasoning

**Q6.** Find the odd one: 8, 27, 64, 100, 125
100 — all others are perfect cubes (2^3=8, 3^3=27, 4^3=64, 5^3=125). 100 = 10^2 (perfect square, not cube).
**Answer: 100**

**Q7.** If MOTHER is coded as KMRFCP, how is FATHER coded?
M→K(-2), O→M(-2), T→R(-2), H→F(-2), E→C(-2), R→P(-2). Same pattern:
F→D, A→Y(-2 wrap), T→R, H→F, E→C, R→P = **DYRFCP**

**Q8.** A is B's sister. C is B's mother. D is C's father. E is D's mother. How is A related to D?
A is B's sister → A and B are children of C → C is D's daughter → A is D's granddaughter.
**Answer: Granddaughter**

**Q9.** Complete the series: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?
Differences: 4, 6, 8, 10, 12. Next term = 30 + 12 = **42**

## English Language

**Q10.** Choose the correct synonym of VERBOSE:
(a) Brief  (b) Wordy  (c) Clear  (d) Concise
**Answer: (b) Wordy** — verbose means using more words than needed

**Q11.** Identify the error: "She don't know the answer to the question."
Error in "don't" — should be "doesn't" (3rd person singular).
**Corrected: "She doesn't know the answer."**

**Q12.** Fill the blank: The committee _____ yet to reach a decision.
**Answer: is** — committee is singular (collective noun treated as singular)

## General Awareness

**Q13.** Which Article of Indian Constitution abolishes Untouchability?
**Answer: Article 17** — abolishes untouchability and forbids its practice

**Q14.** Which gas is used in fire extinguishers?
**Answer: Carbon dioxide (CO2)** — displaces oxygen, smothering the fire

**Q15.** The term "Googly" is associated with which sport?
**Answer: Cricket** — a type of off-break delivery bowled with a leg-break action
