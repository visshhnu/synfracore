# Banking Exams — Overview

## Major Banking Exams in India

India's banking sector recruits through several competitive exams annually. These are conducted by IBPS, SBI, and RBI separately.

| Exam | Posts | Conducting Body | Frequency |
|------|-------|----------------|-----------|
| IBPS PO | Probationary Officer | IBPS | Annual |
| IBPS Clerk | Clerical Cadre | IBPS | Annual |
| SBI PO | Probationary Officer | SBI | Annual |
| SBI Clerk | Junior Associate | SBI | Annual |
| RBI Grade B | Officer Grade B | RBI | Annual |
| RBI Assistant | Assistant | RBI | Annual |
| IBPS SO | Specialist Officer | IBPS | Annual |
| NABARD Grade A | Assistant Manager | NABARD | Annual |

---

## Exam Structure: IBPS PO (Pattern)

**Prelims (Online)**
- English Language: 30 questions, 30 marks, 20 minutes
- Quantitative Aptitude: 35 questions, 35 marks, 20 minutes
- Reasoning Ability: 35 questions, 35 marks, 20 minutes
- Total: 100 marks, 1 hour

**Mains (Online)**
- Reasoning & Computer Aptitude: 45 questions, 60 marks, 60 min
- General/Economy/Banking Awareness: 40 questions, 40 marks, 35 min
- English Language: 35 questions, 40 marks, 40 min
- Data Analysis & Interpretation: 35 questions, 60 marks, 45 min
- Total: 155 questions, 200 marks, 3 hours

**Interview: 100 marks**

Final merit = Mains (80%) + Interview (20%)

---

## Syllabus — Key Topics

### Quantitative Aptitude
Number series, simplification, approximation, quadratic equations, data interpretation (bar/pie/line/table charts), data sufficiency, arithmetic problems (profit & loss, percentage, ratio, averages, time & work, speed & distance, simple & compound interest, mensuration).

### Reasoning Ability
Puzzles and seating arrangements (most important — 15–20 questions), syllogism, blood relations, direction sense, input-output, coding-decoding, inequality, order and ranking, logical reasoning.

### English Language
Reading comprehension (2–3 passages), cloze test, fill in the blanks, error correction, para jumbles, sentence improvement, vocabulary (synonyms, antonyms, idioms).

### General/Banking Awareness
Current affairs (3–4 months before exam), banking basics (RBI functions, monetary policy, NABARD, SEBI, NPA, Basel norms), static GK (headquarters of banks, taglines, MD & CEO), financial schemes (PM Jan Dhan, MUDRA, PM Kisan).

---

## Eligibility

- Age: 20–30 years (relaxation for SC/ST/OBC/PwD)
- Education: Graduation in any discipline from recognized university
- Nationality: Indian citizen

---

## Preparation Strategy

**90-Day Plan:**
- Days 1–30: Concept building (Quant + Reasoning basics)
- Days 31–60: Practice sets + Banking Awareness current affairs
- Days 61–90: Full mock tests daily, error analysis, revision

**Key resources:**
- Mocks: IBPS Guide, Testbook, Oliveboard (attempt 30+ full mocks)
- Banking Awareness: Banking Awareness by Arihant, monthly magazines
- Daily: Read financial news (Economic Times, Business Standard)
