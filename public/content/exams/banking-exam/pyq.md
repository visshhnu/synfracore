# Banking PO/Clerk — Previous Year Pattern Questions

## Quantitative Aptitude

**Q1.** A train 150 m long passes a pole in 15 seconds. How long will it take to pass a platform 100 m long?
(a) 20 sec    **(b) 25 sec**    (c) 30 sec    (d) 35 sec

Speed = 150/15 = 10 m/s
Distance to pass platform = 150 + 100 = 250 m
Time = 250/10 = **25 seconds**

---

**Q2.** Simple interest on ₹8000 at 5% per annum for 3 years is:
(a) ₹1000    **(b) ₹1200**    (c) ₹1500    (d) ₹2000

SI = (P × R × T)/100 = (8000 × 5 × 3)/100 = ₹1200

---

**Q3.** If A : B = 2 : 3 and B : C = 4 : 5, what is A : C?
(a) 8:15    **(b) 8:15**    (c) 6:10    (d) 2:5

A:B = 2:3 = 8:12; B:C = 4:5 = 12:15
A:B:C = 8:12:15 → A:C = **8:15**

---

## Reasoning Ability

**Q4.** In a row of 40 students, Ravi is 12th from the left. What is his position from the right?
(a) 27th    **(b) 29th**    (c) 28th    (d) 30th

Position from right = Total + 1 - Position from left = 40 + 1 - 12 = **29th**

---

**Q5.** Statement: All pens are books. Some books are pencils.
Conclusions: I. Some pens are pencils. II. Some pencils are pens.
(a) Only I follows    (b) Only II follows    **(c) Neither follows**    (d) Both follow

"All pens are books" and "Some books are pencils" — the pencils may be from the non-pen portion of books. Neither conclusion necessarily follows.

## English Language

**Q6.** Choose the word most similar in meaning to ALACRITY:
(a) Laziness    **(b) Eagerness**    (c) Confusion    (d) Sadness

Alacrity = brisk and cheerful readiness/eagerness.

## Banking Awareness

**Q7.** CRR stands for:
**(a) Cash Reserve Ratio**    (b) Credit Reserve Rate    (c) Currency Reserve Ratio    (d) Capital Reserve Rate

Banks must keep a certain % of their Net Demand and Time Liabilities (NDTL) as cash with RBI. Current CRR = 4%.

---

**Q8.** RTGS stands for:
**(a) Real Time Gross Settlement**    (b) Real Time General System    (c) Rapid Transfer Gross Service    (d) Real Trade Gross Settlement

RTGS: high-value transactions (min ₹2 lakh), real-time, one-to-one settlement. NEFT: any amount, batches, net settlement. IMPS: instant, 24x7, up to ₹5 lakh.

---

**Q9.** Which body regulates insurance in India?
(a) RBI    (b) SEBI    **(c) IRDAI**    (d) NABARD

IRDAI = Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India. Headquarters: Hyderabad.

---

**Q10.** Base Rate in banking is:
(a) Interest rate on saving accounts
(b) Rate at which RBI lends to banks
**(c) Minimum lending rate below which banks cannot lend**
(d) Rate on government securities

Base Rate: minimum rate set by each bank individually. Replaced by MCLR from April 2016. Loans are priced at Base Rate + spread.
