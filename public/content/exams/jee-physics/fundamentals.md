## Key Laws and Formulas

```
Mechanics:
  Newton's 2nd law: F = ma
  Work-Energy: W = ΔKE = Fd cos θ
  Conservation of energy: KE + PE = constant
  Momentum: p = mv, F = dp/dt
  Circular motion: a = v²/r, F = mv²/r
  Torque: τ = r × F = Iα

Rotation:
  Moment of inertia: I = Σmr² (discrete), ∫r² dm (continuous)
  KE (rotation): ½Iω²
  Angular momentum: L = Iω
  Parallel axis theorem: I = I_cm + Md²

Electrostatics:
  Coulomb's law: F = kq₁q₂/r²  (k = 9×10⁹ N·m²/C²)
  Electric field: E = F/q = kq/r²
  Gauss's law: ∮E·dA = Q_enc/ε₀
  Capacitor: C = Q/V = ε₀A/d
  Energy stored: U = ½CV² = Q²/2C

Current Electricity:
  Ohm's law: V = IR
  Power: P = VI = I²R = V²/R
  Kirchhoff's laws: KVL (sum of voltages = 0), KCL (sum of currents = 0)
  Resistors in series: R = R₁ + R₂
  Resistors in parallel: 1/R = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂
```
