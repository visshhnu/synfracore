# RRB NTPC — Overview
RRB NTPC recruits for Non-Technical Popular Categories in Indian Railways.
## Exam Pattern
CBT Stage 1: 100 questions, 90 min — Maths(30) + Reasoning(30) + GK(40)
CBT Stage 2: 120 questions, 90 min — Maths(35) + Reasoning(35) + GK(50)
## Key Topics
Maths: LCM/HCF, Percentage, Profit-Loss, Time-Work, Speed-Distance
Reasoning: Series, Analogies, Blood Relations, Directions
GK: Current Affairs, History, Geography, Science, Railways
