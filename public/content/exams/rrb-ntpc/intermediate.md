# RRB NTPC Intermediate Preparation

## Mathematics — Section-wise Strategy

```
SIMPLIFICATION (5-7 questions — easy, do first):
  BODMAS: Brackets, Of, Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction
  Approximation: round to nearest 5 for multiple choice
  Fraction comparison: cross multiply or convert to decimal
  Square roots: learn perfect squares (1-50) for quick identification

NUMBER SYSTEM:
  Divisibility rules:
    2: last digit even | 3: sum of digits divisible by 3
    4: last two digits | 5: ends in 0 or 5 | 6: divisible by 2 and 3
    8: last three digits | 9: sum of digits divisible by 9 | 11: alternating sum
  HCF and LCM: HCF × LCM = product of two numbers
  Remainder theorem: for pattern questions

MENSURATION — IMPORTANT FORMULAS:
  Square: area=a², perimeter=4a
  Rectangle: area=l×b, perimeter=2(l+b), diagonal=√(l²+b²)
  Triangle: area=½×b×h, perimeter=a+b+c
  Circle: area=πr², circumference=2πr
  Cuboid: volume=l×b×h, TSA=2(lb+bh+lh)
  Cube: volume=a³, TSA=6a²
  Cylinder: volume=πr²h, CSA=2πrh, TSA=2πr(r+h)

PROFIT AND LOSS — SHORTCUT:
  Profit% = (SP-CP)/CP × 100
  If profit% = loss% on different items, net result is always loss
  Net loss% = (x²/100)% where x = common profit/loss %
```
