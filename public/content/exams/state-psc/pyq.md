# State PSC — Previous Year Pattern Questions

## General Studies — History

**Q1.** The Quit India Movement was launched in:
(a) 1940  (b) 1941  **(c) 1942**  (d) 1943
Launched on 8 August 1942. "Do or Die" — Gandhi's call at Gowalia Tank, Mumbai.

**Q2.** Who founded the Arya Samaj?
**(a) Swami Dayananda Saraswati** — founded 1875 in Mumbai. "Go back to the Vedas."

**Q3.** The system of Dyarchy was introduced in India by:
(a) Morley-Minto Reforms 1909  **(b) Government of India Act 1919**
(c) Government of India Act 1935  (d) Indian Independence Act 1947

## Indian Polity

**Q4.** The Rajya Sabha is:
(a) Dissolved every 5 years
(b) Dissolved every 6 years
**(c) A permanent House — it is never dissolved**
(d) Dissolved when Lok Sabha is dissolved

Rajya Sabha is a permanent House. 1/3 members retire every 2 years.

**Q5.** Finance Commission is constituted under Article:
(a) 270  (b) 275  **(c) 280**  (d) 285

Article 280: President constitutes Finance Commission every 5 years.

**Q6.** Which Schedule of the Constitution deals with anti-defection law?
(a) 8th  (b) 9th  **(c) 10th**  (d) 11th

10th Schedule added by 52nd Amendment 1985 — disqualifies members who defect from their party.

## Economy

**Q7.** SEBI was established in:
(a) 1988  **(b) 1992**  (c) 1994  (d) 1996
SEBI Act 1992 gave it statutory powers. Was established as a non-statutory body in 1988.

**Q8.** Which Five-Year Plan focused on "growth with social justice and equity"?
(a) 4th  (b) 5th  **(c) 6th**  (d) 7th

**Q9.** The term "Green Revolution" in India is associated with:
(a) Cotton production  (b) Vegetable farming
**(c) Wheat and rice production using HYV seeds**  (d) Organic farming

Green Revolution (1960s-70s): Dr M.S. Swaminathan, Norman Borlaug. HYV seeds + irrigation + fertilisers.

## Geography

**Q10.** The Deccan Plateau is made up of:
(a) Sedimentary rocks  (b) Metamorphic rocks
**(c) Igneous rocks (Basalt/Lava)**  (d) All of the above

Deccan Trap: formed by volcanic activity 60-65 million years ago. Rich in black cotton soil (regur).

**Q11.** Which river is known as the "Sorrow of Bihar"?
(a) Ganga  (b) Gandak  **(c) Kosi**  (d) Sone

Kosi river changes course frequently — devastating floods. Flows from Nepal through Bihar.

## Science & Technology

**Q12.** Ozone layer is found in:
(a) Troposphere  **(b) Stratosphere**  (c) Mesosphere  (d) Thermosphere

Stratosphere: 15-35 km above Earth. Ozone absorbs UV-B and UV-C radiation.

**Q13.** Which vitamin is essential for blood clotting?
(a) Vitamin A  (b) Vitamin C  (c) Vitamin D  **(d) Vitamin K**

Vitamin K: required for synthesis of clotting factors II, VII, IX, X. Deficiency = excessive bleeding.
