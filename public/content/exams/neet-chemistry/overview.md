# NEET Chemistry — Overview

## Chemistry in NEET

Chemistry contributes 180 marks (45 questions) — 25% of total. NEET Chemistry has a good balance of conceptual and numerical questions, making it achievable to score 140+ with proper preparation.

## Three Sections

**Physical Chemistry** (~15 questions): Mole concept, equilibrium, electrochemistry, kinetics, thermodynamics. Numerical-heavy.

**Organic Chemistry** (~18 questions): GOC (General Organic Chemistry), named reactions, mechanisms, biomolecules. Concept-heavy.

**Inorganic Chemistry** (~12 questions): Periodic table, chemical bonding, coordination compounds, p-block/d-block elements. Memory-based.

## Strategy by Section

**Physical Chemistry:** Practice numericals daily. Use formula sheets. Master mole concept first — it's used everywhere.

**Organic Chemistry:** Learn named reactions (Aldol, Cannizzaro, Diels-Alder, Grignard). Understand reaction mechanisms — don't memorize, understand electron flow. NCERT reactions are sufficient for NEET.

**Inorganic Chemistry:** Pure memorization but NCERT coverage is 95%+ sufficient. Use mnemonics for periodic table trends and group properties.
