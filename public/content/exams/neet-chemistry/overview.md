# NEET Chemistry

Chemistry carries 180 marks (45 questions) in NEET. It's divided equally between Physical, Organic, and Inorganic chemistry. Strong NCERT coverage is the key strategy.

## Priority Topics

| Section | Key Topics | Marks |
|---|---|---|
| Physical Chemistry | Mole concept, Equilibrium, Electrochemistry, Thermodynamics, Kinetics | 60-65 |
| Organic Chemistry | GOC, Named reactions, Biomolecules, Polymers | 65-70 |
| Inorganic Chemistry | p-block, d-block, Coordination compounds, NCERT facts | 45-55 |
