# NEET Chemistry — Advanced + Cheatsheet

## Biomolecules

```
CARBOHYDRATES:
  Monosaccharides: glucose (aldohexose), fructose (ketohexose), galactose, ribose
  Disaccharides: sucrose (glucose+fructose, non-reducing), lactose, maltose
  Polysaccharides: starch (amylose+amylopectin), cellulose, glycogen
  Reducing sugars: have free aldehyde/ketone group, give +ve Fehling's/Tollen's
  Test: Molisch (all carbohydrates, purple ring), Seliwanoff (ketoses, red)

PROTEINS:
  20 amino acids, classified by R group
  Peptide bond: -CO-NH- (between COOH and NH₂)
  Primary: amino acid sequence
  Secondary: α-helix (H-bonds within chain) or β-pleated sheet (between chains)
  Tertiary: 3D folding (disulfide bridges, H-bonds, hydrophobic)
  Quaternary: 2+ polypeptide chains (hemoglobin: 4 chains)
  Denaturation: loss of 3D structure (not primary), loss of biological activity

NUCLEIC ACIDS:
  Nucleotide = phosphate + sugar + base
  DNA: deoxyribose, ATGC, double helix, major groove
  RNA: ribose, AUGC, single stranded, has pseudouridine in tRNA
  tRNA: L-shaped, anticodon loop, 3'-CCA accepts amino acid
  mRNA: 5'cap, poly-A tail, codons, UTRs
```

## NEET Chemistry Cheatsheet

```
ORGANIC REACTIONS SUMMARY:
  Alcohols → Aldehyde/Ketone: oxidation (PCC for 1°→aldehyde)
  Aldehyde/Ketone → Alcohol: reduction (NaBH₄, LiAlH₄)
  Alkene → Diol: cold KMnO₄ (syn addition)
  Alkene → Diol (cleave): hot KMnO₄
  Alkene + O₃: ozonolysis (then Zn/H₂O → carbonyl compounds)
  Tollen's test: Ag(NH₃)₂⁺ → Ag mirror (aldehydes only)
  Fehling's test: blue → brick red precipitate (aliphatic aldehydes)
  Lucas test: HCl + ZnCl₂: 3° immediate, 2° slow, 1° no reaction

IMPORTANT COMPOUNDS:
  Bakelite: phenol + formaldehyde (thermosetting polymer)
  Nylon 6,6: hexamethylenediamine + adipic acid
  Teflon: CF₂=CF₂ polymerisation
  Kevlar: aromatic polyamide (bulletproof vests)

p-BLOCK HIGHLIGHTS:
  PCl₅ geometry: trigonal bipyramidal (sp³d)
  SF₆: octahedral (sp³d²)
  ClF₃: T-shaped (2 lone pairs equatorial) sp³d
  XeF₂: linear (3 lone pairs equatorial) sp³d
  BF₃: trigonal planar (sp²), incomplete octet, Lewis acid
  NO₂ is brown (paramagnetic, odd electron) → N₂O₄ (colourless)
```
