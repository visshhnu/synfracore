# SSC CGL Intermediate Preparation

## English Section Strategy

```
COMMONLY TESTED TOPICS:
  Error Spotting: subject-verb agreement, tense errors, article errors
    Subject-verb: singular subject + singular verb (everyone IS, not are)
    Tense: time markers determine tense (yesterday=past, tomorrow=future)
    Articles: a/an/the usage — the before specific, a/an before first mention
  
  Fill in the Blanks: choose grammatically and contextually correct word
    Prepositions: in/on/at (place and time)
    Conjunctions: and/but/or/nor/either-or/neither-nor
    Determiners: few/a few/the few | little/a little/the little

  Synonyms/Antonyms: 2000 most common words for SSC
    Common tested: cogent, benevolent, ameliorate, ephemeral, loquacious
    Learn root words: bene=good, mal=bad, port=carry, dict=say, aud=hear

  Idioms and Phrases (frequently tested in SSC):
    Bite the bullet: endure painful situation | Break the ice: start conversation
    Burn bridges: destroy relationships | Catch red-handed: caught in the act
    Kick the bucket: die | Let the cat out of the bag: reveal secret

  One Word Substitution (high frequency in SSC):
    Ambidextrous: use both hands with equal ease
    Omnivorous: eating all kinds of food
    Insolvent: unable to pay debts
    Misanthrope: hater of humankind
    Philatelist: one who collects stamps

  Reading Comprehension: 4-5 questions from a 200-300 word passage
    Strategy: read questions first, then scan passage for answers
    Time: maximum 5 minutes for complete RC set
```

## General Awareness Strategy

```
STATIC GK HIGH FREQUENCY TOPICS:
  History:
    Ancient: Harappan, Vedic, Maurya, Gupta, Sangam literature
    Medieval: Delhi Sultanate rulers, Mughal Akbar's policies, Bhakti saints
    Modern: Governor Generals, 1857, Congress sessions, important acts, partition

  Geography:
    India: rivers (origin, tributaries), states (capitals, borders), crops
    Physical: Himalayas, Deccan plateau, Thar desert, coastal plains
    World: continents, oceans, largest/smallest/highest/deepest

  Polity:
    Constitutional articles: 12-35 (FR), 36-51 (DPSP), 79-122 (Parliament)
    Important amendments: 42nd, 44th, 52nd, 73rd, 74th, 86th, 101st
    Bodies: UPSC, CAG, Election Commission, Finance Commission, NITI Aayog

  Science:
    Physics: laws of motion, electricity, sound, light
    Chemistry: elements (first 20), acids/bases/salts, periodic table
    Biology: nutrition, respiration, diseases, human body systems

CURRENT AFFAIRS (6 months before exam):
  National: government schemes, budget highlights, awards, appointments
  International: summits, trade agreements, sports events, conflicts
  Science/Tech: space missions (ISRO), defence acquisitions, new tech
  Sports: major tournaments, Indian performance, records
```
