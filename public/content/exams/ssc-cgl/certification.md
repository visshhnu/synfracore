# SSC CGL Certification Guide

## SSC CGL Overview

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Full Name** | Staff Selection Commission Combined Graduate Level |
| **Conducting Body** | Staff Selection Commission (SSC) |
| **Eligibility** | Graduate degree from recognized university |
| **Age** | 18-27 (varies by post), relaxation for reserved categories |
| **Stages** | Tier 1 (CBT) → Tier 2 (CBT) → Document Verification |

---

## Tier 1 Exam (Computer Based Test)

```
SECTIONS (200 questions, 60 minutes, 200 marks):
  General Intelligence and Reasoning: 25 questions, 50 marks
  General Awareness: 25 questions, 50 marks
  Quantitative Aptitude: 25 questions, 50 marks
  English Comprehension: 25 questions, 50 marks

MARKING: +2 correct, -0.5 wrong (negative marking)
CUTOFF: approximately 140-160 for general category

REASONING TOPICS:
  Series (number, letter, figure) | Analogies | Classification
  Coding-decoding | Blood relations | Direction sense
  Syllogisms | Matrix | Venn diagrams | Mirror/water images
  Paper cutting/folding | Seating arrangement | Missing number

GENERAL AWARENESS TOPICS:
  Static GK: history, geography, polity, science, books/authors
  Current affairs: last 6 months, national and international
  Sports: major tournaments, records, Indian players
  Awards: Bharat Ratna, Padma, Nobel, national awards

QUANTITATIVE APTITUDE:
  Number system | Percentage | Profit/loss | Ratio/proportion
  Time/work/distance | Averages | Simple/compound interest
  Mensuration | Trigonometry (basics) | Data interpretation
  Algebra | Geometry

ENGLISH:
  Error spotting | Fill in the blanks | Synonyms/antonyms
  Idioms/phrases | One-word substitution | Sentence rearrangement
  Reading comprehension | Cloze test | Spelling correction
```

---

## Tier 2 Exam (Advanced Level)

```
PAPER 1 (Compulsory for all posts):
  Session 1: Mathematical Abilities + Reasoning + General Intelligence
  Session 2: English Language + Computer Knowledge

PAPER 2 (for specific posts):
  Statistics (for Statistical Investigator/Compiler posts)
  General Studies Finance and Economics (for Assistants in Finance Ministry)

TOP POSTS RECRUITED:
  Group B Gazetted: Assistant Section Officer (MEA, Intelligence Bureau)
  Group B Non-Gazetted: Assistant (Central Secretariat), Inspector (Income Tax, CBI)
  Group C: Sub-Inspector (CBI), Auditor (CAG, CGDA)
```

## Revision Notes
```
SSC CGL: most popular central govt job exam for graduates
4 SECTIONS: Reasoning | GK | Quant | English — equal weight in Tier 1

STRATEGY:
  English and Reasoning: highest accuracy possible (less calculation)
  Quant: learn shortcuts (vedic maths, approximation)
  GK: revise static + current affairs daily (30 min)
  Target: 165-175/200 in Tier 1 for safe cutoff

NEGATIVE MARKING: -0.5 per wrong — skip if less than 60% confident
SPEED: 200 questions in 60 minutes = 18 seconds per question
BOOKS: Rakesh Yadav (Maths) | KD Campus (Reasoning) | Lucent GK | SP Bakshi (English)
```
