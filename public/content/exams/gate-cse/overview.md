# GATE CSE — Computer Science Engineering

GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) CSE is a national-level exam for postgraduate admissions (M.Tech/MS) and PSU recruitment. Score valid for 3 years.

## Exam Pattern

```
Duration: 3 hours
Questions: 65 (10 General Aptitude + 55 Technical)
Marks: 100 total
  - GA: 15 marks (1m × 5 + 2m × 5)
  - Technical: 85 marks (1m × 25 + 2m × 30)
Negative marking: -1/3 for 1 mark, -2/3 for 2 mark
```

## Study Strategy

```
Timeline: 6 months before exam
Months 1-2: Strong subjects (DSA, Algorithms, OS)
Months 3-4: Weak subjects + standard textbooks
Month 5:    Previous year papers (2015-2024)
Month 6:    Full mock tests, revision

Key resources:
  - NPTEL lectures for each subject
  - Standard books: Cormen (Algorithms), Silberschatz (OS/DBMS)
  - GATE Overflow (community Q&A)
  - Previous 10 years papers (mandatory!)

Score needed:
  IITs: 700+ (rank < 500)
  NITs: 500+ (rank < 3000)
  PSUs: Varies by company (DRDO, ISRO, ONGC, etc.)
```
