# NEET Physics — Practice Labs

## Interactive Quiz Sets

The NEET PHYSICS practice labs are quiz-based — use MCQ sets and flashcards for exam preparation.

## How to Use These Labs

1. **Click the Quiz tab** above to start MCQ practice
2. **Answer questions without looking at options first** — write your answer, then check
3. **Review every wrong answer** — understand WHY it's wrong, not just what's correct
4. **Track your score** — aim for 80%+ before your exam

## Tips for Using MCQ Practice Effectively

**Don't rush** — 45 questions in 60 minutes means 80 seconds per question. Practice at this pace.

**Negative marking awareness** — if you're less than 60% sure, skip. -1 for wrong, +4 for correct. Expected value of guessing = (0.25 × 4) + (0.75 × -1) = 0.25. Not worth it.

**Elimination method** — eliminate 2-3 clearly wrong options, then choose from remaining.

**NCERT line questions** — many NEET questions quote exact NCERT lines. Read NCERT with this in mind.
