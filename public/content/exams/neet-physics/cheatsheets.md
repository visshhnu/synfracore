# NEET Physics — Cheatsheet

```
── MECHANICS ────────────────────────────────────────────────
v=u+at, s=ut+½at², v²=u²+2as
Projectile: R=u²sin2θ/g, H=u²sin²θ/2g, T=2usinθ/g
Circular: ac=v²/r, F=mv²/r, Banking: tanθ=v²/rg
Friction: f=μN | Work: W=Fscosθ | KE=½mv² | PE=mgh
Momentum: p=mv | Impulse: J=FΔt=Δp
Torque: τ=rFsinθ=Iα | L=Iω | Conservation if τ=0

── GRAVITATION ──────────────────────────────────────────────
F=Gm₁m₂/r² | g=GM/R² | g_depth=g(1-d/R) | g_height=g(1-2h/R)
Escape velocity: 11.2 km/s | Orbital: 7.9 km/s
T²∝r³ (Kepler's 3rd) | Satellites: T=2π√(r³/GM)

── ELECTRICITY ──────────────────────────────────────────────
F=kq₁q₂/r² (k=9×10⁹) | E=kq/r² | V=kq/r | U=kq₁q₂/r
C=Q/V=ε₀A/d | Series: 1/C=ΣI/Cᵢ | Parallel: C=ΣCᵢ
V=IR | P=VI=I²R=V²/R | Series R=ΣRᵢ | Parallel: 1/R=Σ1/Rᵢ

── OPTICS ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
Mirror: 1/f=1/v+1/u | Lens: 1/f=1/v-1/u | m=-v/u
Snell's: n₁sinθ₁=n₂sinθ₂ | n=c/v
Young's slits: β=λD/d (fringe width)

── MODERN PHYSICS ───────────────────────────────────────────
E=hν=hc/λ | KE_max=hν-φ | λ=h/mv (de Broglie)
Eₙ=-13.6/n² eV (H atom) | rₙ=0.529n² Å
N=N₀e^(-λt) | t½=0.693/λ | A=λN (activity in Bq)

── CONSTANTS ────────────────────────────────────────────────
g=10 m/s² | G=6.67×10⁻¹¹ | c=3×10⁸ m/s | h=6.63×10⁻³⁴ Js
k=9×10⁹ | e=1.6×10⁻¹⁹ C | 1eV=1.6×10⁻¹⁹ J
```
