# NEET Physics

Physics carries 180 marks (45 questions × 4 marks) in NEET. Most students score 120-140 here. Strong physics = competitive advantage.

## Chapter-wise Priority

| Chapter | Expected Questions | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Mechanics (Laws of Motion, Work-Energy, Gravitation) | 10-12 | Very High |
| Electrostatics & Current Electricity | 8-10 | Very High |
| Modern Physics (Atoms, Nuclei, Dual Nature) | 6-8 | High |
| Optics (Ray + Wave) | 5-7 | High |
| Magnetic Effects & Electromagnetic Induction | 4-6 | High |
| Thermodynamics | 3-4 | Medium |
| Waves & Oscillations (SHM) | 3-4 | Medium |
