# GATE ECE (Electronics & Communication Engineering)

## About GATE ECE
GATE ECE is the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering for Electronics and Communication Engineering. It is conducted by IITs and IISc on rotation. Qualifying GATE ECE opens doors to M.Tech/ME/MS admissions at IITs, NITs, IIITs, and PSU jobs (BHEL, BSNL, BEL, ONGC).

## Exam Pattern
| Section | Topics | Marks |
|---------|--------|-------|
| General Aptitude | Verbal, Numerical, Reasoning | 15 |
| Engineering Mathematics | Linear Algebra, Calculus, Probability | 13 |
| Core ECE | See below | 72 |
| **Total** | | **100** |

Duration: 3 hours | Questions: 65 | Negative marking: 1/3 for MCQs

## Core ECE Subjects (Weight)
- Networks: 5-8 marks
- Electronic Devices: 5-8 marks
- Analog Circuits: 8-12 marks
- Digital Circuits: 5-8 marks
- Control Systems: 8-10 marks
- Communications: 8-12 marks
- Signals & Systems: 8-10 marks
- Electromagnetics: 5-8 marks
- Engineering Mathematics: ~13 marks

## PSU Recruitment via GATE ECE
BSNL JE/DE, BEL, ECIL, NHPC, PGCIL, DRDO, and AAI recruit through GATE ECE scores directly.

## Study Strategy
Start with Signals & Systems and Network Theory (these underpin everything), then Control Systems and Communications, then Analog and Digital Circuits. Engineering Mathematics should be practised in parallel throughout.
