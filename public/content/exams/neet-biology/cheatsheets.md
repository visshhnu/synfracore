# NEET Biology — Quick Revision Cheatsheet

```
── CELL BIOLOGY ──────────────────────────────────────────────
Prokaryotic: 70S, no membrane organelles, circular DNA, peptidoglycan wall
Eukaryotic:  80S (cytoplasm), membrane organelles, linear DNA
Mitochondria & Chloroplast: 70S, own DNA (endosymbiont theory)
Lysosome: only animal cells | Chloroplast: only plant/algae
Cell wall: plant=cellulose, fungi=chitin, bacteria=peptidoglycan

── PHOTOSYNTHESIS ───────────────────────────────────────────
PSII (P680): absorbs light → water splits → O₂ + H⁺ + e⁻
PSI (P700):  absorbs light → NADP⁺ → NADPH
ATP made by: chemiosmosis through ATP synthase (F₀F₁ ATPase)
Calvin cycle: CO₂ + RuBP → 3-PGA → G3P → Glucose (stroma)
C₃ first product: 3-PGA | C₄: OAA | Enzyme: RuBisCO

── GENETICS QUICK REFERENCE ────────────────────────────────
Monohybrid F2: 3:1 (phenotype), 1:2:1 (genotype)
Dihybrid F2:   9:3:3:1
Testcross:     1:1 (recessive to find genotype)
Incomplete dom: 1:2:1 (phenotype = genotype)
Co-dominance:  both expressed (AB blood type)
Multiple alleles: ABO blood groups (Iᴬ, Iᴮ, i)
Linkage: genes on same chromosome, violate independent assortment

── DNA / RNA ────────────────────────────────────────────────
DNA: deoxyribose, ATGC, double helix, stable
RNA: ribose, AUGC, single stranded, less stable
A=T (2 H-bonds), G≡C (3 H-bonds)
Chargaff: A=T, G=C | Replication: semi-conservative
AUG = Start | UAA, UAG, UGA = Stop codons
64 codons, 20 amino acids, degenerate and universal

── HUMAN PHYSIOLOGY ─────────────────────────────────────────
Enzymes: Salivary amylase (mouth, pH6.8), Pepsin (stomach, pH2)
         Trypsin, Lipase (pancreatic, small intestine)
Vitamins: A,D,E,K = fat soluble | B,C = water soluble
Vitamin D: calciferol (from sunlight) | deficiency = rickets
Vitamin C: ascorbic acid | deficiency = scurvy
Blood: RBC (no nucleus), WBC (nucleus), Platelets (no nucleus)
Rh: +ve = Rh antigen present | -ve = absent
Universal donor RBC: O negative | recipient: AB positive

── ECOLOGY ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
10% Law: only 10% energy transfers to next trophic level
Pyramid of energy: ALWAYS upright
Mutualism: both benefit (+/+)
Parasitism: parasite+, host- (+/-)
Succession: primary (bare rock) → secondary (disturbed habitat)
Keystone species: has disproportionate effect on ecosystem
```
