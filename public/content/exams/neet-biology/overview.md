# NEET Biology

Biology carries 360 marks in NEET (90 questions × 4 marks) — the single highest-weightage section. Most NEET toppers secure 340+ in Biology. Mastery here is non-negotiable for medical college admission.

## Exam Pattern

```
NEET Biology: 90 questions, 360 marks (out of 720 total)
  Botany: 45 questions (180 marks)
  Zoology: 45 questions (180 marks)
  Correct answer: +4 marks | Wrong answer: -1 mark
```
