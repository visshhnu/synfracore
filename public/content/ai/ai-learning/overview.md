### Module 03 — Flashcard Mode
*Spaced repetition learning*

Flashcards use spaced repetition — concepts you struggle with appear more often. Perfect for memorizing Kubernetes commands, ICD-10 rules, and interview patterns.

**Topics covered:**

- Term → Definition cards — 🟢 Beginner
- Command → Effect cards — 🟢 Beginner
- Scenario → Solution cards — 🟡 Intermediate

### Module 04 — Mock Interview Mode
*Practice PSR answers*

Mock Interview mode presents questions and times your answer. Use the PSR formula for every response. Record yourself speaking — 70% retention from speaking vs 30% from reading.

**Topics covered:**

- Timed answer practice — 🟡 Intermediate
- Topic-specific question banks — 🟡 Intermediate
- Feedback on answers — 🔴 Advanced
