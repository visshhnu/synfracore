# Current Affairs for Banking Exams

## Why This Chapter Matters
Current Affairs (last 6 months before exam) carries 20-25 marks in IBPS PO, 15-20 in SBI PO, 10-15 in Clerk exams. Topics: banking/financial appointments, economic data, summits, awards, government schemes, sports.

## How to Prepare Current Affairs

### Regular Reading Sources
1. **The Hindu** or **Indian Express** — 30 min daily
2. **RBI Monthly Bulletin** — monetary policy, regulations
3. **Economic Survey** (released before Union Budget) — India's economic performance
4. **PIB (Press Information Bureau)** — government scheme launches
5. **Banking Affairs Monthly Magazine** — curated for bank exams

### Topic Categories to Track

#### 1. Banking and Financial Sector
- RBI policy rate changes (Repo rate, CRR, SLR changes)
- New bank licences, mergers of PSBs
- Bank chairpersons and MD/CEO appointments
- Financial regulations (new SEBI rules, NPA guidelines)
- Digital payment milestones (UPI transactions, Fastag)
- World Bank/IMF reports on India's economy

#### 2. National Economic News
- GDP growth quarterly figures
- Inflation data (CPI, WPI)
- Union Budget highlights
- Government scheme launches and targets
- Disinvestment announcements
- FDI (Foreign Direct Investment) figures by sector

#### 3. International News
- G7, G20 summits — key decisions
- India's bilateral agreements
- IMF/World Bank economic forecasts
- Trade deal updates (FTAs)
- UN reports (HDI, World Happiness Index)

#### 4. Appointments
- RBI Governor, Deputy Governors
- Bank Chairpersons and CEOs
- Finance Ministry appointments
- SEBI, IRDAI, PFRDA heads
- Cabinet Minister appointments
- Chief Ministers of states

#### 5. Awards and Honours
- Padma awards (Padma Vibhushan, Padma Bhushan, Padma Shri)
- Nobel Prizes (especially Economics)
- Bharat Ratna (highest civilian award)
- International awards (Ramon Magsaysay, Commonwealth, Forbes lists)
- National Film Awards

#### 6. Sports
- Cricket: ICC rankings, World Cup results, Test series
- Olympics/Asian Games/Commonwealth Games: India's medals
- Key sports appointments (BCCI, IOA)

#### 7. Science and Technology
- ISRO missions (recent launches)
- Defence acquisitions
- India's space achievements
- Major technology policy announcements

### Memory Techniques for Current Affairs

**Grouping:** Link related facts — e.g., all banking appointments together, all scheme launches in one list.

**Mnemonics:** Create short phrases to remember lists (first letters of schemes, names of award winners).

**Revision schedule:** Week 1 topics revised again in Week 3. Month 1 topics revised before exam.

**Test yourself:** Use mock tests with current affairs sections — don't just read, answer questions.

## Typical Question Pattern

**Q type 1 (Appointment):** "Who was appointed as the new RBI Governor in [year]?"

**Q type 2 (Scheme):** "Under PM Mudra Yojana, what is the maximum loan under Tarun category?"

**Q type 3 (Report/Index):** "India ranked ___ in the Human Development Index 2023 report."

**Q type 4 (Organisation):** "The headquarters of ADB (Asian Development Bank) is located in ___."

**Q type 5 (Summit):** "The G20 Summit of 2023 was held in ___." (Answer: New Delhi, India)
