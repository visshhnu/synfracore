# CPT Codes — Intermediate

## Medical Decision Making (MDM) Framework

MDM is determined by 3 elements (must meet 2 of 3 for the level):

**1. Number/Complexity of Problems**
- Minimal: 1 self-limited problem
- Low: 2+ self-limited OR 1 stable chronic condition
- Moderate: 1+ chronic conditions with exacerbation OR 2+ stable chronic conditions OR new problem with uncertain prognosis
- High: 1+ chronic conditions with severe exacerbation OR life-threatening problem

**2. Amount/Complexity of Data Reviewed**
- Minimal/None
- Limited: review of test results, orders, or external records
- Moderate: review of prior external notes, review and order each unique test
- Extensive: independent interpretation of tests, discussion with other providers

**3. Risk of Complications/Morbidity**
- Minimal: OTC medications, minor procedures (no risk)
- Low: Rx drug management, minor surgery with low risk
- Moderate: Rx drug requiring intensive monitoring, minor surgery with risk, elective major surgery
- High: Drug therapy requiring intensive monitoring, decision for hospitalization, emergency major surgery

## Surgical Package Concept

The global surgical package includes all services routinely provided with a procedure:
- Pre-op E/M on day of or day before surgery
- The surgery itself
- Post-op care (0, 10, or 90 days based on procedure)

**Modifier -22**: Unusual procedural services (increased complexity) — requires documentation and increases reimbursement
**Modifier -51**: Multiple procedures — appended to secondary procedures
**Modifier -78**: Unplanned return to OR for related procedure during global period
**Modifier -79**: Unrelated procedure during global period (billed separately)
