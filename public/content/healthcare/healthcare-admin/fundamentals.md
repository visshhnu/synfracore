# Healthcare Administration — Fundamentals

## Overview

Healthcare Administration is a critical component of the healthcare industry, ensuring that medical facilities operate efficiently and that patient information is accurately recorded and accessible.

## Core Concepts

### Healthcare Administration Basics

Healthcare administrators manage the operational, financial, and strategic aspects of healthcare facilities. Key areas: Revenue Cycle Management (RCM), compliance with HIPAA/CMS regulations, staff management, accreditation (Joint Commission), and quality improvement programs.



## Key Terminology

- **Revenue Cycle**: The financial process from patient scheduling through payment\n- **HIPAA**: Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act — protects patient data\n- **CMS**: Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services — primary federal payer\n- **EMR/EHR**: Electronic Medical/Health Record\n- **Prior Authorization**: Insurance approval required before certain services\n- **Explanation of Benefits (EOB)**: Document from insurance showing claim processing
