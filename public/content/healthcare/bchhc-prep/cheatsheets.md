# BCHHC Cheatsheet

## OASIS Timing Quick Reference
| Assessment | Trigger | Window |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| SOC | First skilled visit | Within 5 days |
| ROC | Returns from hospital | Within 24 hours |
| REC | 60-day recertification | Near end of episode |
| SCIC | Significant acute change | Within episode |
| DC | Discharge | At last visit |

## PDGM 5 Factors
| # | Factor | Options |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | Timing | Early / Late |
| 2 | Admission source | Community / Institutional |
| 3 | Clinical group | 12 groups (see below) |
| 4 | Functional level | Low / Medium / High |
| 5 | Comorbidity | None / Low / High |

## Wound 7th Character
| 7th | Use When |
|----|---------|
| A | Active treatment: wound VAC, antibiotics, surgical debridement |
| D | Routine subsequent: dressing changes, normal healing |
| S | Sequela: late effect after acute phase resolved |
