## Articleship (3 Years)

Mandatory practical training under a practising CA:
- Duration: 3 years
- Exposure: Auditing, taxation, accounting, GST returns, company law compliance
- Stipend: ₹2,000–₹15,000/month (varies by city and firm size)
- Big 4 CA firms (Deloitte, EY, KPMG, PwC) offer ₹12,000–₹20,000/month stipend
- Article transfer allowed in certain circumstances
