# Banking & RBI Quick Reference

## RBI Key Rates (Verify current values before exam)

| Rate | Definition | Current Direction |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| Repo Rate | RBI lends to banks | Policy rate (basis for all rates) |
| SDF (Standing Deposit Facility) | Banks park excess cash at RBI (replaced Reverse Repo) | Repo - 25 bps |
| MSF (Marginal Standing Facility) | Banks borrow from RBI at higher rate | Repo + 25 bps |
| Bank Rate | Penal rate for SCBs, long-term | = MSF Rate |
| CRR | Cash with RBI, no interest | Currently 4% |
| SLR | Liquid assets (G-Secs etc.) | Currently 18% |

## NPA Classification

| Type | Overdue Period |
|------|--------------|
| Standard Asset | Fully performing — no overdue |
| Sub-standard | Overdue 90 days to 12 months |
| Doubtful | Sub-standard for more than 12 months |
| Loss Asset | Identified as uncollectable by bank/auditor/RBI |

## Banking Sector Ratios to Know

- **CAR (Capital Adequacy Ratio):** (Tier 1 + Tier 2 Capital) / Risk Weighted Assets — minimum 10.5% in India
- **GNPA Ratio:** Gross NPA / Gross Advances × 100
- **PCR (Provision Coverage Ratio):** Provisions held / Gross NPA × 100 — higher is better
- **CASA Ratio:** (Current + Savings Deposits) / Total Deposits — higher = cheaper funding
- **CD Ratio:** Credit / Deposits — how much of deposits are lent out
- **Net Interest Margin (NIM):** (Interest Income - Interest Expense) / Earning Assets

## Key RBI Departments & Functions

| Department | Role |
|-----------|------|
| Monetary Policy Department (MPD) | Conducts monetary policy, implements repo operations |
| Department of Regulation (DoR) | Issues regulations for banks, NBFCs |
| Department of Supervision (DoS) | On-site inspection and off-site monitoring |
| Financial Markets Operations (FMO) | Open market operations, forex intervention |
| Department of Payment and Settlement | Oversees NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, UPI |
| NPCI (National Payments Corp. of India) | Operates UPI, RuPay, NACH, IMPS |
