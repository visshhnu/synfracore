## Quick Legal Maxims for Viva

| Latin Maxim | Meaning | Application |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| Ubi jus ibi remedium | Where right, there remedy | Every right has a remedy |
| Nemo debet esse judex in propria causa | No one should be judge in own cause | Natural justice |
| Audi alteram partem | Hear the other side | Natural justice |
| Ex turpi causa non oritur actio | From a disgraceful cause, no action arises | Illegal contracts |
| Ignorantia juris non excusat | Ignorance of law is no excuse | Criminal liability |
| Actus reus non facit reum nisi mens sit rea | Act not criminal without guilty mind | Criminal law |
| In pari delicto potior est conditio possidentis | Equal fault = possessor prevails | Illegal contracts |
| Res ipsa loquitur | The thing speaks for itself | Negligence — evidence |
| Volenti non fit injuria | Consent is a defence | Torts |
| Damnum sine injuria | Damage without legal injury | No remedy |
