## What is Law & Compliance Awareness?

Every professional should understand their employment contract. Key areas: notice period obligations, non-compete clauses (enforceability in India), statutory benefits (PF, gratuity, ESI), and intellectual property assignment.

## Why Law & Compliance Awareness?

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act regulates how personal data is collected and processed. GDPR applies if you handle EU residents' data. Understanding data classification, consent, and breach notification is professional necessity.

## Learning Modules

### Module 01 — Workplace Rights
*Employment law basics*

Every professional should understand their employment contract. Key areas: notice period obligations, non-compete clauses (enforceability in India), statutory benefits (PF, gratuity, ESI), and intellectual property assignment.

**Topics covered:**

- Employment contract essentials — 🟢 Beginner
- Notice period and termination — 🟡 Intermediate
- PF, ESI and statutory benefits — 🟡 Intermediate

### Module 02 — IT & Data Laws
*PDPB, IT Act, contracts*

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act regulates how personal data is collected and processed. GDPR applies if you handle EU residents' data. Understanding data classification, consent, and breach notification is professional necessity.

**Topics covered:**

- India PDPB (Personal Data Protection) — 🟡 Intermediate
- IT Act 2000 and amendments — 🟡 Intermediate
- GDPR basics for global teams — 🔴 Advanced

### Module 03 — Documentation & Compliance
*Contracts, NDAs, agreements*

Non-disclosure agreements protect confidential information in both directions. SLAs define service obligations and penalties. Open source licenses matter — GPL is copyleft (your code becomes GPL), MIT/Apache are permissive (commercial-friendly).

**Topics covered:**

- NDA key clauses — 🟡 Intermediate
- Service level agreements — 🟡 Intermediate
- Open source license types — 🔴 Advanced
